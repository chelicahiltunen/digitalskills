Services

Pet Visits

$15 per visit.

Some pets are happiest to stay in their home. Depending on your pet's needs (walk, litter change, play in the yard), visits last between 25-40 minutes.

All visits include taking out the trash, watering plants, and collecting mail.

There is no charge for additional pets.

Pet Boarding

When visits are not enough, we welcome your pet into our home.



Boarding for dogs: $50 per day.



Cats and other small animals: $25 per day.

About M & P

Michele and Peyton are a mother-daughter petsitting team serving the greater lansing area.

Michele has worked with animals and in animal rescuse for the past 18 years. Recently she assited the Lansing Humane Society with rehoming and rehabilitating 250+ cats rescued from a hording situation.

Michele also has opened her home to several special needs animals. She is experienced giving medicaiton, sub-Q fluids, wound cleaning, and dressing changes.

Peyton loves helping her Mom care for their own and other people's pets, including her dog Marco. Peyton enjoys taking Marco swimming and on walks.
